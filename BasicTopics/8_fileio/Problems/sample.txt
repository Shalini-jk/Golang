India is the seventh-largest country in the world by land area and the second-most populous country.
New Delhi is the capital city of India, and it serves as the political and administrative center.
India's diverse geography includes the Himalayan mountain range, fertile plains, and a vast coastline.
The Indian subcontinent has been home to ancient civilizations, including the Indus Valley Civilization.
India gained independence from British rule on August 15, 1947, following a non-violent struggle led by Mahatma Gandhi.
The national flag of India consists of horizontal stripes of saffron, white, and green, with a navy blue Ashoka Chakra in the center.
